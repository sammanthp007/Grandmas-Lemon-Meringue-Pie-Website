About Us

We started when Mrs. Granny Smith started making pie for the local community on sundays as part of her hobby to cook pies, in 1934. Since then, her family has supported her venture to open up a business and expand the reach of her tasty pies in the belly of 10000 cities. This expansion shows our dedication to consistantly provide finger licking tasty pies. Our current mission statement is

To achieve a society where people start refering to Pies as Granny's pie and actually start calling Pies as Granny's.

Currently, we have stores open in 2000 cities and we provide online delivery to 10,000 cities globally.
